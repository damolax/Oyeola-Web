Healthcare service

The simple story

Problem: The business needed more than an online brochure. Visitors needed to understand the service area, medical provider options, booking path, FAQs, and how to contact the team.

What I improved:

  • Created a clear website structure for patients and providers.
  • Added booking, FAQ, resources, and contact pathways.
  • Organized service-area and operation-hour information.
  • Made the site feel more trusted and easier to navigate.

Outcome: A clearer website experience for people who need mobile phlebotomy services and want to know what to do next.

What visitors needed

A fast way to understand the business, trust it, and take the right next step.

What the site needed

Clear sections, simple words, proof, FAQs, and visible action buttons.

Why it matters

When a website explains the business clearly, people do not have to guess before contacting or buying.
