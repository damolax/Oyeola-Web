Shipping Website With Step-by-Step Flow, Service Cards, FAQs, and Quote CTA

A shipping website that turns a complex process into simple steps, service options, trust points, and action buttons.

View live workBack to portfolio
Shipping and logistics

The simple story

Problem: Shipping can feel complicated. Visitors needed to understand how the process works, what services are available, and how to start.

What I improved:

  • Created a simple step-by-step explanation.
  • Added service cards for shipping options.
  • Included trust numbers, testimonials, and FAQs.
  • Built CTAs for registration and quote requests.

Outcome: A clearer journey for people who want to shop, ship, and understand the next step.

What visitors needed

A fast way to understand the business, trust it, and take the right next step.

What the site needed

Clear sections, simple words, proof, FAQs, and visible action buttons.

Why it matters

When a website explains the business clearly, people do not have to guess before contacting or buying.
