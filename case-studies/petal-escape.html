Commercial Floral Design Website With Industry Pages and Estimate CTA

A service website built to show commercial plant and floral solutions for hospitality, healthcare, corporate, and retail spaces.

View live workBack to portfolio
Commercial floral design

The simple story

Problem: The business needed a clear way to explain its commercial design service across different industries and guide visitors to request an estimate.

What I improved:

  • Organized the service around clear industry use cases.
  • Added benefit sections for commercial spaces.
  • Created clear CTAs for estimate and contact.
  • Made the page easier for people and search tools to understand.

Outcome: A clearer service website that explains who the business serves and what action to take.

What visitors needed

A fast way to understand the business, trust it, and take the right next step.

What the site needed

Clear sections, simple words, proof, FAQs, and visible action buttons.

Why it matters

When a website explains the business clearly, people do not have to guess before contacting or buying.
