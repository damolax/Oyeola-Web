Process

A simple process for turning business confusion into a clearer system.

Every project starts by understanding what is unclear, what is leaking opportunities and what needs to work better.

The goal is not to add more tools. The goal is to make the business easier to understand, easier to track and easier to grow.
01

Discovery

You send the website, store, workflow or business problem. I ask only what is needed to understand the situation.

02

Diagnosis

I identify what is unclear, missing, manual, untracked or blocking enquiries, sales or follow-up.

03

Structure

I map the improved page, workflow, tracker, dashboard or conversion path before building.

04

Build & handoff

You get a practical setup and simple guidance so it is easy to use after launch.

What I look for

I check the whole path, not only the page.

A visitor sees your website, understands or gets confused, takes action or leaves, then your business either follows up properly or loses the opportunity. That whole path matters.

✓
Website clarity

Can people understand what you do in a few seconds?

✓
Trust and proof

Do visitors see enough reason to believe you?

✓
Next step

Is it obvious what they should do next?

✓
Follow-up

Does your business know who to contact and when?
